Chicken Tortilla Soup

A creamy, spicy, comfort soup that is great for all times of the year

This is a meal my family prefers during the colder months of the year, but is a great comfort food for any month. I get asked constantly to make this meal, the family never seems to get tired of it.


A simple recipe that is best served with chicken breast, plenty of cheese, tortilla chips, and fresh cilantro to cohesively combine for a delicious filling family dinner or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound chicken breast
  • 1 10.5 oz can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 small can of mild red enchilada sauce
  • 1 small can of rotel
  • 1 can of black beans drained and rinsed
  • 1 bag of frozen corn kernels
  • 1 container of chicken bone broth
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 yellow onion
  • Cilantro, sour cream, and tortilla chips for topping
